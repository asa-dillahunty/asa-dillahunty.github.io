Capture the Flag

Rules

Two players start on opposite sides of the playing field a few blocks in front of thier flags. The objective is simply to touch the other player's flag. Players have 3 options.

  • Move
    • A player can move either left or right at the cost of 1 plus thier change in height.
  • Dirt
    • A player can move a single block of dirt from thier immediate left, right, or their own position, to either of those three positions at the cost of 1 turn.
  • Bomb
    • A player can throw a bomb to destroy terrain anywhere on the map at the cost of the floor of distance divided by 10 plus the radius of the explosion raised to 1.5.
