What can I do?

The Tuscaloosa Children's Center, Inc. is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization. Monetary donations are needed to meet the costs of operation. We raise from 1/4 to 1/3 of our operating funds each year. Your cash contribution helps us to pay our bills and to provide direct client services.
